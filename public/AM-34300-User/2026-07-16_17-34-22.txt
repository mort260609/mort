  Hi Matthew,
  
  Thank you again for your reply and for being open to considering alternative technologies.
  
  After reviewing the approved designs together with our development team, we've taken the time to evaluate not only the implementation effort, but also the long-term technical strategy for the project.
  
  Rather than simply recommending a CMS, our goal was to identify the architecture that would provide the best balance of performance, maintainability, scalability, and overall business value over the coming years.
  
  Based on our review, we believe a modern headless architecture is the strongest fit for this project.
  
  ---
  
  # Our Recommendation
  
  After evaluating multiple implementation approaches, we recommend building the website using **Strapi** as the headless CMS together with a modern frontend framework.
  
  From our perspective, this provides significant advantages over a traditional CMS implementation while giving you complete ownership of your infrastructure and data.
  
  We've evaluated two approaches that we believe are the strongest candidates.
  
  ---
  
  # Option 1 – Astro + Strapi
  
  Based on the current project scope, this is our recommended solution.
  
  Your website is primarily a corporate marketing website focused on:
  
  - content presentation;
  - SEO;
  - performance;
  - user experience;
  - long-term maintainability.
  
  Rather than a highly interactive web application.
  
  Astro is specifically designed for this type of project.
  
  Combined with Strapi it provides:
  
  - Self-hosted CMS with full ownership of your infrastructure and data.
  - Outstanding SEO performance.
  - Extremely fast page loading.
  - Minimal JavaScript delivered to the browser.
  - Modern, clean architecture.
  - Reusable page templates and structured content models.
  - Excellent scalability.
  - Easier future integrations.
  - Lower long-term maintenance costs.
  
  Because Astro renders static pages while loading JavaScript only where required, it delivers exceptional performance for corporate websites.
  
  ---
  
  # Option 2 – Nuxt + Strapi
  
  Nuxt is another excellent option.
  
  We would recommend Nuxt if you expect the website to evolve significantly over time.
  
  Examples include:
  
  - advanced interactive modules;
  - client portals;
  - dashboards;
  - complex search & filtering;
  - application-like frontend functionality;
  - additional products connected to the website.
  
  Nuxt offers greater frontend flexibility while still providing excellent SEO and performance.
  
  ---
  
  # Why We Recommend This Architecture
  
  Compared to a traditional CMS implementation, this approach provides:
  
  - Better website performance.
  - Faster page transitions.
  - Better Core Web Vitals.
  - Cleaner architecture.
  - Greater scalability.
  - Easier future integrations.
  - Full ownership of your data.
  - Modern development workflow.
  - Lower long-term maintenance costs.
  
  Overall, we believe this architecture better matches both the current scope of the project and its potential future evolution.
