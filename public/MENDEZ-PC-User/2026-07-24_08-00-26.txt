  ## 1. What NEXAI Is
  
  A dual-token protocol on Base (Ethereum L2):
  
  - **$NEXAI** — onchain AI-sector index token. Deposit approved
    tokens, mint $NEXAI representing a proportional share of a basket
    of AI-sector crypto assets (currently 2 tokens on testnet — WETH/
    USDC placeholder — real basket will eventually hold up to 20 AI
    tokens). Dynamic supply (expands/contracts with deposits/redeems),
    no fixed cap. In-kind mechanism: no swaps or price oracle involved
    in the core mint/redeem math.
  - **Governance token** (name TBD) — separate ERC-20, 100M fixed
    supply. Linear (token-weighted) voting — not quadratic, that was
    reconsidered and dropped for a real mathematical reason (see
    Section 4). Fixed-window token freeze during votes. Separate
    staking-based fee reward layer, independent of voting weight.
    Vesting for Team/Ecosystem allocations via Sablier (cancelable
    streams).
  
  Already deployed and tested on Base Sepolia testnet:
  ```
  NexaiIndexToken: 0x1C87f4647C0B946A28739725A7F67E94967bbd18
  NexaiCore: 0xB324A0B49dD60f5c1e783DCffbF71ABB368B0c19
  ```
  Governance token contracts are built and tested but not yet deployed
  for real — treat as "coming soon" in any UI that references it.

  the real production version, per the client.
